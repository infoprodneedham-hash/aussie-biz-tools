Your Data is Yours

We fiercely value your privacy. BizToolHub does not track, collect, or transmit your personal information to any servers. All data is processed and stored exclusively within your device's local browser memory.

Data Loss Warning

Your records only exist on the specific device and browser you are using. If you wipe your browser cache, site data, or history, your records will be permanently erased. We strongly advise manually backing up critical data.

Estimator Use Only

These free applications are designed to act as administrative guides and estimators only. You must manually fact-check and verify all calculations, outputs, and generated logs before relying on them.

No Professional Advice

BizToolHub is a self-managed utility suite. It does not replace certified financial, legal, or medical advice. By using this software, you acknowledge that you assume all responsibility and liability for its use.
